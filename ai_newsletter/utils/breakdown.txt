automated ai newsletter: 

what this does: 
- collects a list of user's email addresses through an intake form (tkinter, sheety)
- scrapes 5 news sources for AI realted articles and collects their contents using beautiful soup 
- summaarizes the news sources (https://freeapitools.dev/) 
- sends an email summarizing AI related news from the top 5 largest tech news sites to all the users (smtplib) 
- logs the emals in a spreadsheet (sheety) 

when run: 
- the appication scrapes 5 news sources for AI related arricles and collects their contents. 
- the contents are summarized and then sent to the users in the database via email. 
- the tkinter app launches to add a user's email address
